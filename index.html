Who We Are

SetaX is an online digital studio specializing in the design and development of websites, mobile applications, and games. Our fully remote team of talented designers and developers works together to create modern, functional, and visually engaging digital experiences. At SetaX, we are passionate about empowering women and youth in technology by providing them with the opportunity to work remotely and grow professionally. We believe in combining creativity with purpose — turning every idea into a unique and impactful product. We focus on helping small businesses, shops, and local organizations establish and expand their digital presence through smart, customized solutions.

Our Mission & Vision

At SetaX, our mission is to empower creativity and innovation through technology — giving individuals and businesses the tools to thrive in the digital era.

  • To empower women and youth to work remotely and achieve financial independence through tech.
  • To design and build websites, apps, and games that are modern, accessible, and beautifully crafted.
  • To help small businesses and communities grow by strengthening their digital presence.

Our vision is to create a global network of creative professionals working together — remotely, flexibly, and passionately — to shape the future of digital experiences with purpose and heart.
